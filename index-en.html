OPERATING PROCEDURE OVER THE CREATION OF THE SITE

Formulation of the problem
We put your tasks and wishes in the first place and seriously approach each project, whether it be a business card site or a large trading portal.
Marketing research
Before developing the project, we analyze the sites of competitors and select the most effective options for the structure and presentation of information.
Concept development
Specialists create a layout indicating the main sections, demonstrating the layout of the site and with the justifications transfer the project for approval to the client.
Website creation
We develop a web project “from scratch”: from concept and design to programming, connecting tools and filling content.
Testing and launch
Before loading the site, the hosting server checks it for the absence of bugs, cross-browser compatibility and correct display on any devices.
Training
We demonstrate to your employees all the features of the site, quickly and easily explain how to work with the admin panel and track the necessary information.
Support and Promotion
If you wish, we continue to “take care” of your site: we monitor the relevance of the content, update the software part, connect new tools and attract new users.
